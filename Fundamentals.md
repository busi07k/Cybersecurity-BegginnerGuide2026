This path is designed to be followed in order. Do not skip to the end. Here is why you need each of these steps:

1. **Networking Fundamentals:** You cannot attack or defend a network if you don't know how computers communicate. This is where you learn the language of the internet: IP addresses, ports, DNS, OSI model...
2. **Windows Fundamentals:** Over 90% of corporate environments run on Windows and Active Directory. You must understand its internal processes, registry, and permissions to know how companies actually work.
3. **Linux Fundamentals:** Most web servers and almost all cybersecurity tools run on Linux. You need to lose your fear of the black terminal and learn how to navigate the command line natively.
4. **Cybersecurity Fundamentals:** Before picking a team, you need to speak the terminology. Here you will learn the core concepts: SOC, SIEM, malware types, phishing, and vulnerability management.
5. **Choose your path:** Once your foundation is solid, it's time to make a decision. We will guide you to your next specific step: Blue Team or Red Team.

## 1. Networking Fundamentals

As we mentioned in the index, you cannot secure or hack a network if you don't understand how it works. **Rule #1:** Do not touch any hacking tools yet (like Nmap or Wireshark). Focus purely on understanding how computers talk to each other.

* **Watch (YouTube):** [Professor Messer - Free CompTIA Network+ Course](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLG49S3nxzAnlCJiCrOYuRYb6cne864a7G) 
> *(Highly recommended: If you want to validate your knowledge and boost your resume, the **CompTIA Network+** is one of the best entry-level certifications you can get. This playlist covers exactly the theory you need).*
*  **Practice (TryHackMe):** [Network Fundamentals Module](https://tryhackme.com/module/network-fundamentals) 
*(Complete exactly this module: What is Networking, Intro to LAN, OSI Model, and Packets & Frames. Build the base first).*
*  **Read (HackTheBox Academy/Free Alternative to Tryhackme):** [Introduction to Networking](https://academy.hackthebox.com/module/details/34) 
*(A very solid, text-based module to reinforce the OSI model and TCP/IP. It is completely free in their Tier 0).*

**If you complete this successfully, you will have a rock-solid foundation in networking.**

## 2. Windows Fundamentals

## 4. Cybersecurity Fundamentals

This is the final step of your foundation. Here, you will learn the "why" and "how" of security: the CIA triad, types of malware, social engineering, how a Security Operations Center (SOC) works... 

* 🔴 **Watch (YouTube):** [Professor Messer - CompTIA Security+ Training Course](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLG49S3nxzAnkL2ulFS3132mOVKuzzBxA8)
> **Pro Tip:** The **CompTIA Security+** is the most recognized entry-level certification in the world. If you want to bypass HR filters and land your first job, I highly recommend studying this material and taking the official exam. It is the best way to prove you have the technical knowledge.

* 🟢 **Read (HTB Academy):** [Information Security Foundations](https://academy.hackthebox.com/module/details/73)
